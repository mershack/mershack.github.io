mokoe001 at fiu edu

I am a PhD Candidate in the School of Computing and Information Sciences (SCIS) at FIU .

I am a member of the Information Visualization group, and my advisor is Dr. Radu Jianu.

My research work explores evaluation of visualization systems, and the use of Crowdsourcing to simplify the evaluation process.

Before joining FIU, I obtained an MSc. in Advanced Computing from University of Bristol in the UK, and a BSc. in Computer Science from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Ghana.
